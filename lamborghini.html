WELCOME TO LAMBORGHINI

Some of the exterior features of the LAMBORGHINI URUS are given below:

  • Powered by a 4.0-liter twin-turbo V8 engine producing 650 CV and 850 Nm of torque
  • Rear diffuser
  • Spoiler lip

The customisation option for the Urus has been created by Lamborghini’s Centro Stile design department and includes a two-tone exterior with the brand’s high-gloss four-layer pearl colours of Giallo Inti, Arancio Borealis and Verde Mantis in combination with high gloss black roof, rear diffuser, spoiler lip and other details.

PRICE : R3 495 000
